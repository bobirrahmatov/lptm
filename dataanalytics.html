Empowering Lending Through Data-Driven Intelligence

The Loan Product & Transaction Management (LPTM) Data Analytics initiative transforms how Wealth Lending Products are supported by turning raw data into actionable insights. Through the integration of OneWealth, the Regional Credit Platform (RCP), and leading tools like Tableau and Dataiku, LPTM delivers greater transparency, enhanced control, and operational excellence across the lending lifecycle.
