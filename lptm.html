Our Teams

Driving control, efficiency, and excellence across every stage of the lending process

Within Wealth Loan Product & Transaction Management (LPTM), six dedicated teams collaborate to deliver a seamless and controlled lending experience. Each team plays a crucial role in managing data integrity, operational processes, client service, and deal execution â€” ensuring that every stage of the loan lifecycle is executed with precision, transparency, and compliance.

Credit Management Reporting

Ensures comprehensive oversight and transparency across the credit lifecycle through data analysis, dashboards, and regulatory reporting. Focuses on delivering timely, accurate, and actionable insights that support management decision-making and risk control.

Learn more

Credit Information Management

Maintains and governs critical credit data to ensure accuracy, consistency, and compliance with internal and regulatory standards. Drives the implementation of data quality controls and promotes data integrity throughout the credit process.

Learn more

Credit Service Management

Supports front-to-back coordination of credit operations and ensures seamless service delivery to clients and stakeholders. Acts as a central point for issue resolution, escalation management, and process efficiency improvements.

Learn more

Deal Closing

Manages the end-to-end process of loan and letter of credit closings. Performs feasibility checks, compiles documentation packages, and facilitates coordination between stakeholders to ensure timely, compliant deal execution.

Learn more

Loan Management

Oversees the full lifecycle of loan operations, from onboarding to repayment. Ensures accuracy in loan bookings, monitors transactions, addresses client escalations, and strengthens accountability through continuous process improvements.

Learn more

Credit Approval Control

Supports the credit approval process by verifying data quality, ensuring policy adherence, and managing approval workflows. Focuses on embedding robust controls to maintain operational integrity and reduce risk across all credit approvals.

Learn more

The LPTM Central Hub serves as a single access point for all information, tools, and resources related to Loan Product & Transaction Management. It brings together documentation, processes, governance materials, and contact channels to ensure consistent access and collaboration across all regions.

The hub provides direct access to:

  • LPTM Process & Control Documents
  • Governance & Reporting Frameworks
  • Training Materials and Reference Guides
  • Data & Reporting Standards
  • Escalation and Contact Directory

Use the Contact Form provided on this page to submit:

  • Operational queries
  • Deal lifecycle support requests
  • Data/reporting issues
  • Escalations or process clarifications

Your request will be routed to the appropriate LPTM function for prompt handling.
